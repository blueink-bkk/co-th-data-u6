# Relay boards

With 4 to 6 miniature relays, rating 16A 250V, coil 220/240V. 
One of their application is to convert single pole devices into 3 poles.  
Minimum size: 37 x 115 x 19.4 mm.  
Maximum size: 37 x 115 x 19.4 mm.  
Material: PCB.  
Type: Relay boards.
