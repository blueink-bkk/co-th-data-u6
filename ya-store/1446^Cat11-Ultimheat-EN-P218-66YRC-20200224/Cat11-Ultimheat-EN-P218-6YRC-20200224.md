# Relay boards

With 2 to 3 miniature relays, rating 16A 250V, coil 220/240V. One of their 
application is to convert single pole devices into 3 poles.  
Minimum size: 38 x 60 x 19.4mm. 
Maximum size: 31 x 70 x 19.4mm.  
Material: PCB.  
Type: relay boards.
