# Steatite connection blocks 450V range. Protected against accidental electric contact, 
stamped brass terminals, nickel plated steel screws.

Applications: This series has versions with direct pressure screws and indirect 
clamping by stainless steel pressure plate, more suitable for flexible and extra-flexible conductors.  
Ceramic: Steatite type C221, unglazed, slightly creamy color.  
Dielectric strength: higher than 3000V. Minimum insulation distance through ceramic between 2 terminals: 2mm.  
Screw: Galvanized steel 4.8, reduced diameter slotted cylindrical head, according to DIN 920.  
Terminals: CuZn40Pb2 brass, high mechanical strength.  
Maximum operating voltage: 450V, in pollution class 3.
